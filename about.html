About us

Furniture, founded in 2004, is one of the largest and most powerful private enterprise groups in China's furniture industry.

 Furniture, founded in 2004, is one of the largest and most powerful private enterprise groups in China's furniture industry. Based on the furniture industry, the group is involved in many fields such as industrial investment, furniture, real estate projects, etc. The group is headquartered in Dongguan City, Guangdong Province. It has self built modern factory buildings and administrative office buildings. It has a spacious and bright workplace, green trees, a clean and hygienic living environment, and a comfortable and freehand leisure, entertainment and cultural atmosphere, forming a modern garden style work industry 。
